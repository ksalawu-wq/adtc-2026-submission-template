Incident Response Playbook for African SMEs

PHASE 1 - IDENTIFICATION
- Review IDS/SIEM alerts for anomalies
- Classify severity: Critical, High, Medium, Low
- Document: source IP, destination, time, affected systems

PHASE 2 - CONTAINMENT
- Isolate affected systems from the network immediately
- Block suspicious IPs at the firewall
- Preserve logs before any remediation

PHASE 3 - ERADICATION
- Remove malware or unauthorised access
- Reset compromised credentials
- Patch exploited vulnerabilities

PHASE 4 - RECOVERY
- Restore from clean backups
- Monitor restored systems for 72 hours

PHASE 5 - LESSONS LEARNED
- Document full incident timeline
- Update firewall rules and access policies
- Train staff on what triggered the incident

NIGERIAN CONTEXT: Report incidents to ngCERT (cert@certncc.gov.ng) and NDPC if personal data was involved.
